How To Play FROGGER

Help froggy across the road avoiding the racing cars. Use the arrow keys to move and cross the river by jumping on logs.

About FROGGER

Frogger is a 1981 arcade game developed by Konami. It was licensed for North American distribution by Sega-Gremlin and worldwide by Sega itself. It is regarded as a classic from the golden age of video arcade games, noted for its novel gameplay and theme. The object of the game is to direct frogs to their homes one by one by crossing a busy road and navigating a river full of hazards. Enjoy!
